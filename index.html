Personal Details

I have a BSc in Computer Engineering in Instituto Superior de Engenharia do Porto (ISEP) and I’m currently finishing a MSc in Software Engineering at the same institution in a post-work regime.
The most enriching component of the BSc was the fact that, during three semesters, I was part of a new ISEP pedagogical project called CDIO-IL. In this project the students are challenged to develop throughout the semester in parallel with the curricular units, a technology-based project proposed by a company. The development was performed by teams organized according to the SCRUM methodology. This was my first experience with this agile methodology.
Also, during my Computer Engineering degree I had training in Android development by the IT Engineering Department. In total, I developed five apps: one application for evaluation, two others for the CDIO-IL and two for my own.
I'm interested in technological challenges with potential to create positive impact on society. My future ambitions are to enrich my knowledge and capacities for productive development of applications of considerable size and application.
